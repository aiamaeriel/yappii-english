About Aia

Hello learners! I studied BS in Business Administration, majoring in Marketing Management, and I'm TESOL/TEFL certified. Since then, I've been passionately pursuing my career in English teaching.

I aim to improve your language skills without breaking a sweat, all within a fun and supportive environment. Let's make learning English an exciting journey where you feel confident and motivated every step of the way!
